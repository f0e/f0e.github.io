hlae

hlae configs for recording using ffmpeg and streams.


download

last update: 11 aug 2021


options

recording options can be edited in hlae_settings.cfg

commands

  • rec - start recording
  • end - end recording
  • toggle_players - toggle hiding players

configs

note: all of these configs require hlae_base.cfg and hlae_settings.cfg.
  • hlae.cfg
    • regular recording
  • hlae_blur.cfg
    • regular recording with motion blur
  • hlae_vm.cfg
    • regular recording - records game and viewmodel streams into seperate files
  • hlae_adv.cfg
    • advanced recording - records game, depth, viewmodel and hud streams into seperate video files
