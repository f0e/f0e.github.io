hlae configs

hlae configs for recording high-quality CS:GO clips using ffmpeg and streams.

record motion blurred gameplay, separate the game, viewmodel and hud into their own video files and more.

available configs

note: all of these configs require hlae__base.cfg and hlae__settings.cfg.
  • hlae.cfg
    • regular recording
  • hlae_blur.cfg
    • regular recording with motion blur
  • hlae_vm.cfg
    • records game and viewmodel streams into separate files
  • hlae_vm_blur.cfg
    • records game and viewmodel into separate files with motion blur
  • hlae_adv.cfg
    • records game, depth, viewmodel and hud streams into separate files
